Grammar Resources are helpful in almost all scenarios. Learning grammar in a foreign language has proven to be one of the hardest aspects. With a bunch of different tenses, one is bound to be confused. Below is a compilation of Grammar Resources people have found useful in their studies.

List of Grammar Resources

  • About.com French

    This site is a favorite among French students. It offers grammar, vocabulary, and interesting articles about culture.

  • Bonjour de France

    An education "cyber-magazine" which provides exercises, tests, and games for French learning as well as teacher resources.

  • Études Littéraires

    Aimed at native French students, but great for those wishing to expand their knowledge of grammar and literature studies.

  • Lawless French

    A well designed website offering level-based exercises and lessons.

  • Tex's French Grammar

    A pedagogical reference for grammar that combines explanations with surreal dialogues and cartoon images.

  • Wikipedia Article: French Grammar

    This resource is for those who wish to read an overview of French grammar.
